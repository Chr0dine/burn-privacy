Privacy Policy for Burn

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Burn ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Burn, we collect the following information:

Data TypeWhat We CollectPurpose
Account InformationName, email addressTo create and manage your account, and for authentication
Third-Party Sign-InApple ID or Google account identifiers (if you choose to sign in this way)To authenticate you and let you sign in without creating a separate password
ImagesProfile photo; photos you attach to step logs and goal logsTo personalize your profile and to let you visually track your progress within the app

How We Store Your Information

We use Google Firebase as our backend infrastructure provider. Specifically:

  • Firebase Authentication handles sign-in and securely manages your credentials, including Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google where applicable.
  • Firebase Storage stores the images you upload (profile photo, step log photos, goal log photos).
  • Cloud Firestore stores your account details and app data (such as your name, email, and logs) in a structured database.

Your data is stored on Google's secure cloud infrastructure. Google acts as our data processor, and their handling of data is governed by their own security and privacy practices.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Create and authenticate your account
  • Provide core app functionality, including displaying your profile and logs
  • Let you track steps and goals over time, including any photos you attach
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the app

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your images or personal data for advertising.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With Google/Firebase, solely as our infrastructure provider, to deliver the app's functionality
  • If required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Burn, our users, or others

Data Retention

We retain your account information and uploaded images for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will delete your associated data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal or security purposes.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can update your name, profile photo, and other account details within the app at any time.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address below.
  • If you signed in with Apple or Google, you can manage or revoke the app's access to that account through your Apple ID or Google Account settings.

Children's Privacy

Burn is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Security

We rely on Firebase's industry-standard security practices, including encrypted data transmission, to help protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
